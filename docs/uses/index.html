Possible uses of MarketPixel

#Project overview:

The main functionality of the application is:

  • Identify a product
  • Identify contents of a product
  • Get personalized alerts about the product
  • Compare product to other similar products by plotting.

#Product identifier

Product identifier allows the user to understand what product they are looking at. The product identification process is in two phases:

  1. Label/name of the product is translated into user preferred language.
  2. Contents as well as nutrition information of the product are identified and translated.

#Product alerts

User can set alerts on products. For example, user defines they have allergy to peanuts, then when a searched product is found to contain peanuts, an alert is displayed to the user. Alerts can be:

Custom ingredient based

#Product comparison plot

Products can be compared to other products in same category by their nutritional values. Furthermore, all products have nutri-score attached to them, so user of the application can find the highest quality product for them. Users have the possibility to compare products on plot based on products nutri-score, sugar-, protein-, fiber-, sodium-, energy- and fat contents.
